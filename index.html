HI! I’M EDREI!

I’m a Computer Science graduate from Cavite State University–Main Campus, passionate about exploring the power of data and how it drives decisions, sparks innovation, and unlocks new possibilities. I transform complex datasets into actionable insights and I have deep love for uncovering hidden patterns and telling compelling stories with data.

My journey into data began during my internship in UI/UX, where I saw how even the smallest insights can transform user experiences. That spark turned into a fire when I worked on my thesis: “Beyond the Flames: Exploring the Role of Oxygen Concentration, Wind Speed, Humidity, and Temperature in Fire Behavior”, a project where I built a simulation model using Random Forest Algorithm, FDS, and SMV, combined with real-life fire experiments and sensitivity analysis on environmental datasets to discover which variable affects fire behavior the most.

Today, I channel that same curiosity into analyzing data, uncovering patterns, and finding stories hidden in numbers. For me, data isn’t just technical, it’s about curiosity, discovery, and impact.
